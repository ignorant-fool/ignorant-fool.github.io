"Summer."

You know, I hear that Japan has some truly horrendous summers, but being Australian, it's kinda hard to image it being worse than here? Like yeah sure it can get stupidly hot and humid there, but it's like that here *and* we're still in Winter?
Anyway, as always we are behoved to use the Japanese seasons when discussing our favourite animated Chinese cartoons. At the end of this *Summer* season, let us give some thoughts on the animations we've watched over the past few months.

I will start with the not-currently airing anime and the OVA/specials I watched, followed by the Summer season anime in roughly worst-to-best order. Note that these are my thoughts and opinions, so don't get too mad (or do, fight me). I'll give each of the currently airing shows a one-word rating from the following, along with some brief discussion/notes.
    - dropped: I dropped it for whatever reason
    - incomplete (___): this the first cour of a multi-season show, but will give it a preliminary rating in brackets
    - average: perfectly okay, nothing spectacular
    - good: I enjoyed the show
    - great: I really enjoyed the show
    - AOTS: My choice for Anime of the Season!

Although it should be obvious, this is your only spoiler warning!
